🗑️ Delete Your Account — Fit Note

You can delete your Fit Note account and all associated data directly from the app at any time.

How to delete your account

1. Open Fit Note
2. Go to Settings (⚙️ icon)
3. Tap Account
4. Tap Delete my account
5. Confirm with your password

What gets deleted

✅ Your account and login credentials
✅ All workout sessions and history
✅ Programs and training plans
✅ Weight tracking data
✅ Personal records (PRs)
✅ Morphological profile
✅ Badges and achievements
✅ Coach conversation history

Data retention

All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. No data is retained after deletion.
