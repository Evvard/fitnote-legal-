🗑️ Delete Your Data — Fit Note

You can delete specific data without deleting your account.

How to delete specific data

1. Open Fit Note
2. Go to Settings (⚙️ icon)
3. Tap Data Management
4. Tap Delete my data
5. Select which data to delete
6. Confirm deletion

Data you can delete

✅ Workout history
✅ Weight tracking
✅ Personal records
✅ Custom exercises
✅ Programs
✅ Morphological profile
✅ Badges and achievements
✅ Coach conversation history
